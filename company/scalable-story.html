OUR STORY

History


Founded in 2023, Scalable Solutions originated in San Diego, California, and quickly expanded to Riverside. Driven by a steadfast commitment to providing expert-level IT services and customer support, our company has grown rapidly, offering a wide range of tailored technology solutions to meet the evolving needs of our clients.


Our growth is a testament to our dedication to leveraging a broad spectrum of open-source technologies to create valuable software solutions for businesses. Scalable Solutions excels in developing data management systems and full-stack web applications, offering a diverse range of services that include AI, automation, networking, digital marketing, e-commerce, cybersecurity, and more. With a strong foundation in both technical expertise and business acumen, we are positioned as a leading provider of tailored technology solutions. We are committed to enhancing business processes and workflows, continuously innovating to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our Values


MISSION: At Scalable Solutions, our mission is to empower individuals, businesses, and communities by providing first-class service and leveraging our technical expertise. We are passionate about strengthening the hardworking middle class, delivering innovative and tailored technology solutions that enhance efficiency, security, and growth. Our commitment is to put power back into your hands, enabling you to thrive in a dynamic and ever-evolving digital landscape.


VISION: At Scalable Solutions, our vision is to create a community where integrating advanced technology into your business and daily life is seamless and accessible to everyone. We strive to ensure that individuals and smaller companies receive first-class access to the latest innovations without having to rely on impersonal tech giants. We envision a world where you don't have to be an expert to leverage cutting-edge technology, enabling you to focus on what matters most while we handle the complexities of your tech needs.

Our CEO


Michael Johnson:


As the CEO and Co-Founder of Scalable Solutions, Michael Johnson brings a unique blend of technical expertise and business acumen to our team. Self-taught in multiple programming languages, Michael has developed a wide range of skills, from building data management systems to full-stack web applications. His journey includes co-founding and serving as CTO of Belto AI, where he contributed to advancing artificial intelligence technologies. Alongside his hands-on technical experience, Michael holds a degree in Business Administration from San Diego State and is on track to receive an MBA in Information Systems from UC Riverside in 2025. His dual background in technology and business enables him to understand and optimize business processes effectively, driving our mission to empower individuals, businesses, and communities with first-class tech services and expertise.
